# Privacy Policy for MyAnimeListSync

**Last updated:** 3/10/2026

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how MyAnimeListSync ("the Extension", "we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our extension to add MyAnimeList tracking capabilities to Seanime.

## Relationship with Seanime

**Important:** This extension is an independent, third-party plugin for Seanime. We are **not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected** to the Seanime project or its developers. Our extension simply enables Seanime users to synchronize their anime/manga with MyAnimeList.

## Information We Collect and Process

### Information You Provide

- **MyAnimeList authentication tokens**: When you connect your MyAnimeList account, we obtain OAuth tokens necessary for API access.
- **Seanime configuration**: Basic information about your local Seanime instance to enable communication between the two services.

### Information from MyAnimeList

Through the official MyAnimeList API, we may access:

- Your anime and manga lists
- Your username and public profile information
- Your tracking status, scores, progress, and episodes watched

### Local Storage

The Extension stores locally on your device:

- Authentication tokens (securely, using Seanime's storage APIs)
- Extension preferences and settings
- Temporary cache of your list data to improve performance

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

- Enable synchronization of your anime/manga progress between Seanime and MyAnimeList
- Display your MyAnimeList data within the Seanime interface
- Provide the core functionality you expect from the extension

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### MyAnimeList API

Our extension communicates directly with the **official MyAnimeList API**. Your use of the extension is subject to MyAnimeList's:

- [API License and Developer Agreement](https://myanimelist.net/static/apiagreement.html)
- [Terms of Use](https://myanimelist.net/about/terms_of_use)
- [Privacy Policy](https://myanimelist.net/about/privacy_policy)

### Seanime Communication

The extension communicates with your **local Seanime instance** (running on your own device). All communication happens locally on your Seanime installation—**no data is sent to our servers**.

### No Third-Party Sharing

We do not:

- Sell your personal information
- Share data with any third parties (except as required for MyAnimeList API functionality)
- Use your data for advertising, analytics, or marketing
- Maintain external servers that collect your information

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- All API communications use HTTPS encryption
- Authentication tokens are stored securely using your Seanime's secure storage APIs
- We follow security requirements outlined in the MyAnimeList API Agreement
- No passwords are ever stored—only OAuth tokens

## Your Rights and Choices

You can:

- **Disconnect** your MyAnimeList account at any time through the extension settings
- **Uninstall** the extension to remove all locally stored data
- **Manage** your MyAnimeList data directly through your MyAnimeList account settings
- **Contact us** with any privacy concerns

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date and through the extension's distribution channel (GitHub and Seanime marketplace).
